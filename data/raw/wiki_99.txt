Davyd Arakhamia

David Heorhiyovych Arakhamia, also known by the pseudonym David Braun, is a Ukrainian politician and entrepreneur of Georgian origin. He is a member of the Servant of the People political party. Arakhamia was elected to the Verkhovna Rada in 2019. In parliament, he was elected as his party's faction leader on 29 August 2019.
