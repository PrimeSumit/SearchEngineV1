Teresa Palmer

Teresa Mary Palmer is an Australian actress. Following her film debut in Wolf Creek (2005), Palmer had supporting roles in films such as The Grudge 2 (2006), Bedtime Stories (2008), The Sorcerer's Apprentice (2010), I Am Number Four, Take Me Home Tonight, Knight of Cups (2015), Triple 9 (2016), The Choice (2016), and The Fall Guy (2024).
